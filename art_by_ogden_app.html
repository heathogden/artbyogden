Tax notes

Prices are tax-inclusive at the booth. Tax to remit is back-calculated as gross × rate / (1 + rate). Current rate: 7.65% (Orem, Utah County). Change TAX_RATE at the top of the script if you sell in a different city — e.g., West Jordan was 7.45%. The Day Summary in the Tracker tab shows the back-calculated tax to remit at the current rate.

Tax collected is not income — it must be remitted to the Utah State Tax Commission. Bank statement is the source of truth for payment-method totals; use it (not the Tracker) to reconcile after the event.
